Decisions

IDChoice · Rationale
D-001Default to weekly. Saved-link volume needs enough density for a newspaper read.
D-002Keep daily as an in-view toggle. The same renderer supports a tighter morning-paper mode.
D-003Expire by rolling off the paper, not by deleting rows. Existing board, list, and search remain the permanent archive.
D-004Let createNewspaperView own article clicks. The renderer already owns edition controls and can ignore source links locally.

Tradeoffs

IDPicked over Rejected · Reason
T-001client-side edition over daily_pages. The user asked to see links themselves, not an LLM-generated brief.
T-0027-day rolling weekly over calendar-week only. It avoids an empty Monday edition after a quiet weekend.
