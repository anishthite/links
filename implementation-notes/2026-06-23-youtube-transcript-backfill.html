D-001Run sequentially with sleep between notes. Small corpus; easiest way to avoid rate-limit spikes.
D-002Backfill only notes whose source_content_text is empty. Skip good rows.
D-003Sync transcript updates to prod with one SQL file after local success. Fewer remote writes.
D-004Fallback to YouTube description/title when transcript body is unavailable. Better than leaving video notes blank.
